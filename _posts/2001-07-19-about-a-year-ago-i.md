About a year ago, I found a website called [Stratfor][1] that I enjoyed reading tremendously, for its mostly unbiased and informative news updates. It makes me feel like a lot less of a dope than I do when I try to glean a global picture from, say, [CNN][2].

However, I lost the URL for this site for quite sometime, only to find it again, and discover that it's now a pay site as of 2000 ($79.99/yr). However, on the recommendation of a friend I have found a site that might be even better, and has a great selection of interesting and informative articles that read more like papers than articles. Check it out: <http://www.indymedia.org/>
