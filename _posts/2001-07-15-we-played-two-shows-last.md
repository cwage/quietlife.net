We played two shows last week. One at [The End][1] on Thursday, and one at Sebastian's and Diana's Brew Pub on Friday.

They went well, but we need to work on getting crowds that contain people other than our friends. We also need to work on getting opening bands that don't drive those crowds away.

Aaron made the observation that he feels like we're a real band now that we've played two shows in a row. Rock on.
