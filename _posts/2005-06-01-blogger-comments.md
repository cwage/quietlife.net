I have noticed a lot of blogs lately I've been reading that require a blogger account to make a comment.

Sorry, folks, I am not going to bother. Take it from me, as a representative sample of the prototypical lazyass reading your blog.

For the brief duration that I required typekey authentication on my blog for comments, I .. didn't get any.

I suggest you allow anonymous comments or whatever blogger lets you get away with.
