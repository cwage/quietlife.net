So, here's something fun for Friday. In high school and intermittently during college (well, what college I actually bothered with), I was in a band. We were called Agent Eight -- I played bass and sang on some songs. It also featured Aaron Hock on drums, Steve Linn on keys, and Daniel Hearn on vox/guitar.I have a ton of mp3s from various times in our rock music career, but I thought I'd toss a few up here. Most of these were recorded in or around 1998 and 1999 -- either live at clubs or in practice sessions with a little mini-boombox or a dictaphone mic (yes, we took our sound engineering seriously). We have other, better recordings we did digitally/multitrack that are great documentation that I would make a bitchin recording engineer, but we never really finished them, and so they aren't as fun to listen to. The quality is horrible, but you can still hear the rocking:
