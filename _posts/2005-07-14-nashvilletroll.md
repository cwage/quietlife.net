Okay, I have a theory. Glen Dean and his blog [Nashville Truth][1] are not real, and are, in fact, a splendid [troll][2].

As far back as May, even, I once commented to a friend that I found [this post][3] rather ironic, given the "commentary based in fact, rather than emotion" subtitle. Maybe I was more right than I knew.

Take for example [this post][4], its [comment thread][5] (on which I eventually took the bait), and also a [discussion thread][6] over at [Nashville is Talking][7]. All the signs are there. The inflammatory tone. The frequent misspellings. The constant redirection away from any semblance of debate back into ad-hominem insults directed at whoever is silly enough to engage him.

Looking back on it, I feel silly that I didn't realize this earlier. Good show, Glen!
