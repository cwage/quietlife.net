**"[Jim Giles][1], White Patriot versus Chip Pickering, White Christian-Republican Whore."**

Now **there's** a campaign slogan. I stumbled across this guy's site, and I've been entranced by it all morning. I thought it was parody at first, but a quick [google search][2] reveals that it's quite real. Jim Giles is an independent repeat candidate for Mississippi's 3rd congressional district. He wants to represent "White People, Dirt White People", by leading a "Rebel Army" of "White Patriots".

From what I can gather, Jim Giles is a: racist (anti-semite and anti-african american), anti-immigration, Christian, anti-free trade, anti-Iraq War, pro-life, anti-taxes, Confederate sympathizer. Phew. Talk about an enigma wrapped in a riddle.

Here are some choice samples from his website:
