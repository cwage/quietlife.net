We had a nice afternoon on Sunday at my parents' place for mother's day.

Amanda and I went to the store and bought stuff to make a big meal. We made a huge giant pot of chicken cacciatore.

The appetizer we made is one of our new favorites -- "insalata caprese" which translates roughly to "salad of capri". It's just really really fresh mozzarella sliced in half, tomatoes (we used cherry tomatoes), fresh basil, and olive oil. [epicurious.com][1] says not to use any balsamic vinegar because it would "destroy" the taste of the mozzarella, but we added a splash with the olive oil. What do they know anyway?

 [1]: http://www.epicurious.com/
