If I was living in district 21, I think I'd have a hard time voting for Douglas Henry. The most I know about [Douglas Henry][1] is that he really hates it when people stand on the state seal. Oh, and he's "genteel". I think I'd consider holding my nose and voting for [Bob Krumm][2]. Even though I disagree with 75% of what he stands for, I at least know what that stuff is. Could I ignore the political ramifications? Could I vote for a Republican? Could I vote for someone that I agree with 25% of the time over .. a guy that is "genteel"? Well, probably not. This is what drives voter apathy, people. Fortunately for me, I'm not in district 21, so I'll be spared this moment of cognitive dissonance.
