So, [last.fm][1] is pretty cool. I've now been tracking my listening habits for well over a year -- since March 2005. You can view the [overall charts][2] for everything I've been listening to since then, as well as [more recent][3] data.

What does this tell me about my musical taste? Well, more than anything, it verifies that I am pretty compulsive in my music-listening -- as in over a year, I've hit only a small segment of the music I'd tell anyone else I "listened" to. But clearly that's not true. I listen to new stuff. Over, and over. And over.
