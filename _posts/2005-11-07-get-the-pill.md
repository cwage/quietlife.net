While [we're all sitting around waiting][1] for the FDA to stop stalling on approving Plan B, [www.getthepill.com][2] is providing an alternative:

> If you are 18 years or older and need a prescription for emergency contraception (the morning after pill), please complete our on-line medical questionnaire.
> 
> A licensed OB/GYN will review your submitted medical information to determine if you are an appropriate candidate for emergency contraception.
> 
> If approved, we will submit your prescription to a local US pharmacy of your choice. 

Interesting idea. Of course, this won't help if the pharmacy you choose is staffed by a cretin [incapable of doing his job,][3] but it's something.
