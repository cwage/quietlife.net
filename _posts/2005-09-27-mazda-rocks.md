[Mazda has beaten][1] the rest of the auto industry to the punch by eliminating four harmful heavy metals from all of its cars -- lead, hexavalent chromium, cadmium and mercury. This is no surprise, considering the [new Miata][2], which is bigger, badder, better and faster, and still only 22lbs heavier than the previous generation. You can't do that with lead, no sir.
