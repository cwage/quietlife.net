In the past when I searched on [ebay][1] for random parts for my car, I always found ads for various "xenon", "HID", "blue" and such lights for my car.

I had no idea what any of it meant until someone pointed me at [Daniel Stern Lighting][2], which has everything you'd ever want to know about car headlights (mostly).

Now I know that more expensive "xenon" bulbs might help a little, but not much, and that HID lights are more expensive to install than they are probably worth.

I also have a scientific background to make fun of ricers with blue headlights trying to imitate HID lights.
