My Canon Powershot A520 broke. [Again.][1]

I am not pleased. Last time, the lens cover jammed shut. I shipped it to Canon and they fixed it, which was nice. This time it jammed open, which you might think is better because I can still take pictures -- except for the part where I can't actually take it anywhere for fear of scratching the lens worse than it's probably already going to get scratched on the return trip to Canon.

Canon at least is making me happy by being gracious about repairing it again -- this time they sent me a shipping label so I didn't have to pay for shipping. My [D30][2] can't come soon enough.
