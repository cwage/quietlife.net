[auto.com][1] has [an article][2] about the new Jaguar S-type R.

It looks pretty beefy -- supercharged 4.2L V8 churning out nearly 400HP.

But they didn't put a limited slip differential on it, which means it could never seriously compete with the M5 as far as super sport sedans are concerned. But Jaguar says they didn't intend for it to..

At least they're making something that isn't a glorified Taurus now..
