I left my [Livejournal URL][1] lie dormant since I moved to WordPress. I used to keep it synchronized for the convenience of my LJ-using friends, but that was a pain with wordpress so I never bothered, since people could just as well read the [syndicated URL][2]. However that bugs me a little because I have no control over it, and sometimes the syndication screws up the HTML and I can't fix it.

So, anyways, I installed [this plugin][3], which didn't exist at the time. Let's see if this works.

**UPDATE:** Looks like it worked. So, LJ readers, if you want to read my blog in LJ, the [chrismwage][1] user is back in action.
