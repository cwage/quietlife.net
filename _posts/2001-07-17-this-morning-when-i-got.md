This morning, when I got out of the shower, still quite dreary-eyed from sleep deprivation and dreading the workday ahead, I noticed the words "I LOVE YOU" scrawled on the mirror so they wouldn't show up until it got steamy -- a clever little love letter from my girlfriend.

You have no idea -- the effect something like this can have on a bad mood.
