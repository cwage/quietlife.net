I wanted to rent [Rosencrantz & Guildenstern Are Dead][1] tonight, but Blockbuster didn't have it. I was bummed

It's a great movie with [Gary Oldman][2], [Tim Roth][3], and [Richard Dreyfuss][4], based on the [novel][5] by [Tom Stoppard][6].

It's a sortof surreal novel written around the plot of Hamlet, and the two characters, Rosencrantz, and Guildenstern.

I can't even begin to describe what it's about, but it's great. Read it and then rent it.
