We saw [Munich][1] this evening. I found it to be pretty tedious. The over-arching point of the movie seems to be: the Israel/Palestine conflict is fucked up (subpoint: violence sucks). I guess maybe if you are a person whose moral framework is still unclear on those points, 3 hours of people being shot and blown up will really shake your worldview, but frankly I found it to be a little boring.
