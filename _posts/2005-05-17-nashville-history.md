Brittney [links to][1] some good historic photos of Nashville. As I've mentioned there as well, this guy's site is pretty incredible, with lots of [recent stuff][2] as well. My favorite though is his [Nashville Memories][3] page which documents a bunch of stuff that I am entirely too young and too-originally-from-wisconsin to remember, but it's interesting nonetheless.

And a very brief book recommendation: If you are into local history as I am, Bill Casey's "Fortunes, Fiddles and Fried Chicken: A Business History of Nashville" is a fascinating book. I think you can still get copies of it at Davis Kidd.
