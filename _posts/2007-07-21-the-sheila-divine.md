Last night at Rumours, in between bottles of overpriced wine, I was talking to a girl who was in school at Berklee around the same time I was in Boston, and we were swapping stories about bands and venues we used to frequent. I was surprised that she had heard of [The Sheila Divine][1], a band I had befriended while I was up there -- but I guess I forgot how popular they were up there for a while. I still have a sealed copy of their [first single][2] laying around somewhere.

Anyways, I was browsing around and I found the [totally awesome home page][3] I made for them back in the day.. (The archive.org cache is from 2001, but it must have been around 1998 that I made the page). Behold my tremendous web-design skillz.

It made me miss Boston a lot. I need to get back up there to visit.
