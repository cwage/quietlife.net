There's an exhibition next Friday that will include a silent auction to benefit a memorial fund in Will's name. It's Friday, July 21 from 7-11PM. Mark your calendars. Here's the full announcement:

<!--more-->

  
OFF THE WALL PRESENTS:  
"PORTAL SKIN"  
ONE NIGHT ONLY- FRIDAY, JULY 21, 2006

OFF THE WALL members and featured guest artist Abby  
Whisenant, will be exhibiting new work in their sixth  
show of the series, "Portal Skin." Members include  
Nashville- based artists, Jenny Baggs, Quinn Dukes,  
Janet Heilbronn, Mahlea Jones, Jenn Ramsey, Jaime  
Raybin, and Iwonka Waskowski. New paintings,  
installation, photography, silk screen prints and  
sculpture will be exhibited. A silent auction will be  
held to benefit the Will ClenDening Memorial Fund.  
This one night exhibition is free and open to the  
public. The exhibition will take place FRIDAY, JULY  
21, 2006 FROM 7-11 PM AT MARATHON VILLAGE (1305  
CLINTON ST.)

ABOUT OFF THE WALL:  
Off The Wall is a group of independent artists  
committed to supporting one another through dialogue  
and opportunities to exhibit artwork. While working  
individually members are brought together by common  
aesthetic sensibilities and an interest in developing  
contemporary approaches in the visual arts. Through  
presenting new work, Off The Wall seeks to build a  
reciprocal relationship with the viewer. Each artist  
displays multiple pieces in order to give the viewer a  
meaningful look into their unique body of work.  
Additionally, as members of the Nashville community,  
Off The Wall supports local non-profit organizations  
through the auctioning of artworks and donating sales. </blockquote>
