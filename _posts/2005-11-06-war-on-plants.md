An encouraging setback for the war on drugs in Denver:

> DENVER, CO - Voters in Denver have passed a measure that makes it legal for residents over the age of 21 to possess up to an ounce of marijuana.
> 
> Initiative 100 won by a margin of 53% to 46%.
> 
> Opponents have said that it will have no practical effect because the city ordinance will be trumped by Colorado state law. 

So, no practical ramifications, and the law passed by a very narrow margin, but still, it's encouraging.
