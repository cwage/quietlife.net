> The organization A Million Voices for Darfur is attempting to collect 1 million "postcards" urging President Bush to support a multinational force to protect the people of Darfur to present by April 30th. If you'd be interested in getting involved in a more direct way, there is an organization called Tennesseans Against Genocide. You can get in touch with them by emailing Dr. Daniel Schafer at <schaferd@mail.belmont.edu>.
