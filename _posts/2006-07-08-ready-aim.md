I admit I haven't been following the Senate campaign very closely, because it's not a huge priority for me. You all know I'm not Harold Ford's biggest fan, but he's still got my vote, by virtue of running as the unopposed Democrat candidate.

But I can't help watching the [antics surrounding the Republican opposition][1] and being reminded of the cliche about a circular firing squad. It looks like Corker, Hilleary and Bryant might all take eachother out before Ford even gets a chance.
