[Parke Wilde][1] is guest-blogging over at [CalorieLab][2], along with Tanya Taylor of [IAteAPie.net][3] (best domain name ever), who posts [news of a new sweetener, tagatose][4]. There's more info about it [on wikipedia][5], but the highlights appear to be that it's a naturally occuring monosaccharide similar to sucrose, but with 1/3 the calories. It's stable enough to bake with, and the glycemic impact makes it safe for diabetics.

Looks like this may be a pretty tempting alternative for a low-calorie sweetener for those that are turned off by the idea of [chlorinated sugar][6]. But come on, a little chlorine never hurt anyone, right?
