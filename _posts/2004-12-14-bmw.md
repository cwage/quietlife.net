I think the BMW I loved is slowly dying.

From [autoblog][1], pictures of [the new BMW M6][2]. Still boring as ever. Saaay, that's a nice .. [mercury cougar][3]? Yes it's a marvel of engineering, but it's **boring**.

And this reviewer is [not impressed][4] with BMW's new 1-series:

> Park one of these on your drive and the neighbours will not think, &#8220;Hmm, that&#8217;s an expensive car. He must be doing well.&#8221; They&#8217;ll think, &#8220;Hmm, that&#8217;s an expensive car. He must be off his rocker.&#8221; You can have a Golf GTI for less, and that, in almost every single way, is a better car. 
> 
> So if you want a hatchback, buy a Focus. If you want a hatchback with some go, buy the VW. If you just want some action and you don&#8217;t care about space in the back, or a hard ride, or the price, buy a sports car. A Honda S2000 would be fine. 
> 
> I have been accused, in recent years, of having it in for BMW. There was even some talk, after my recent review of the dreadful X3, that I would not be allowed any more press demonstrators. And this is why I&#8217;ve devoted the entire column this week to a test of the 1-series rather than tagging it on to the end of a rant about cheese. 
> 
> And it&#8217;s why I&#8217;m choosing the words for my conclusion with even more care than usual. So here goes. The 1-series is crap.

Ouch.
