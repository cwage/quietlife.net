In other news, we went camping at [Fall Creek Falls][1] this weekend, where I took a [crapload of pictures][2]. Finally, I found a use for my wide lens that yielded some results I was happy with. Although [this one][3] seems to be the most popular, I liked this one a lot:

[<img src="http://static.flickr.com/93/251934937_95f5e6190d.jpg" width="333" height="500" alt="Fall Creek Falls" />][4]

The weather was beautiful all weekend until Saturday night. We had been getting little squalls blowing past here and there all day, and then a dark cloud moved in -- another little storm that I was sure would blow over. Well, it did blow over. 13 hours later. One thing that would be nice to have in the woods is radar.
