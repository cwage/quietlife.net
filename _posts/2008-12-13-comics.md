Pursuant to a conversation last night, a list of web comics I read that you should also be reading because they are awesome. Listed vaguely in order of awesomeness:

  * [Achewood][1]
  * [Dinosaur Comics][2]
  * [Pictures for Sad Children][3] -- when I discovered this comic I was so happy I could cry.
  * [Perry Bible Fellowship][4] -- updated rarely, but rarely unfunny.
  * [Cat and Girl][5] -- one of my old favorites, though I will admit it can be hit or miss. A few years ago I listed [some of my favorites][6].
  * [Penny Arcade][7] -- despite the fact that I never play video games, I still enjoy Penny Arcade, because it is hilarious.
  * [Boy on a Stick and Slither][8] -- cute, hit or miss, and occasionally hilarious.
  * [xkcd][9]
  * [Saturday Morning Breakfast Comics][10] -- just discovered this one. It's fairly funny, despite the author's obvious issues with women and premature ejaculation.
