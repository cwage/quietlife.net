Well, we just saw [Napoleon Dynamite][1]. There's 82 minutes of my life I'll never get back. It was awful.

It struck me as a [Rushmore][2] rip-off by someone that didn't grasp why Rushmore was funny -- that you weren't supposed to be laughing **at** the main character, but **with** him.
