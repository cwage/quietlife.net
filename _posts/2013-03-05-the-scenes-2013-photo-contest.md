Some of the photos from the [Scene's photo contest][1] that I thought were noteworthy:

  * <a rel="lyteshow[frontpage]" href="http://www.nashvillescene.com/imager/scott-simontacchi-eloise-bowling-avenue/b/original/3298119/44fd/scottsimontacchi.jpg">Scott Simontacchi, “Eloise,” Bowling Avenue</a>
  * <a rel="lyteshow[frontpage]" href="http://www.nashvillescene.com/imager/holden-head-pasiphae-east-nashville/b/original/3298133/d452/Holden_Head_3.jpg">Holden Head, “Pasiphae,” East Nashville</a> -- I actually found this photograph to be grotesque, but that doesn't make it a bad one. It did get me reading about Pasiphae. Pasiphae, in Greek mythology, was the daughter of Helios that fucked a bull and gave birth to the Minotaur, and represented the evils of feminine lust and excess. I am not 100% certain what the connection between her and this photograph is, but it got me thinking/reading, so there's that.
