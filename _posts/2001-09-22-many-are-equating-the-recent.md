Many are equating the recent attacks on the World Trade Center to an act of war the magnitude of which we have not seen since Pearl Harbor.

They believe that a sleeping giant has again been awakened.

Maybe so.

However, I would remind those calling for immediate and vengeful war that sleeping giants wake up disoriented and drowsy.

I would also remind those making war with that giant that sleeping giants wake up cranky and bad-tempered.
