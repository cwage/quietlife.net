A few weeks ago I bought a bottle of campari on a whim and spent the weekend drinking it with soda.

I spent this weekend compulsively listening to [Ennio Morricone][1]'s soundtrack to "[Il Gatto][2]", which is really quite lovely.

It would have been fitting to have combined these two activities into one weekend, I think.

I also downloaded Morricone's score to [Cinema Paradiso][3], which is also amazing. It's reminding me how much I like file scores -- particularly the 60s-70s spy/lounge music sort -- and also, only slightly tangentially, how much I want to get my hands on all things Henry Mancini. Unfortunate, [emusic][4] has no Mancini whatsoever, despite having nearly everything Morricone ever did.
