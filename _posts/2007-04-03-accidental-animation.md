I will start writing again soon, I swear, once I can work up a sweat about anything other than vitriolic bitterness or wallowing self-pity. In the meantime, more photocrap.

I spent around 30 minutes tonight trying to capture lightning as the storm blew in. While I got some spectacular shots of the pensive nothingness just **before** a large, spectacular, awe-inspiring, room-shaking lightning strike, I only actually caught it once or twice. Upon review, however, it occurred to me that instanding there taking exposure after exposure, I had accidentally created a nice set of pictures for an animation.
