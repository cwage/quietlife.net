In discussion with some friends just now, including one that [just had a lung transplant][1], we were talking about organ donation and what you had to do to signal your intent. I checked the little box and whatnot on the back of my drivers' license, but it was mentioned that probably the best way is just to make sure your family and friends know you wanted it.

So, I figure, what better authoritative record is there than my blog? After I die, they can just google "chris wage" "organ donation".

So, let it be known: I want to be an organ donor. Take it, I don't need it!
