Having a place to live is pretty cool. I like it a lot. In this mayoral election, it may seem like things like convention centers and baseball stadiums are really important. But, they're really not. Having a place to live is. I'd encourage you to focus on an issue that is thankfully getting some well-needed attention: Nashville's affordable housing situation, and the [Mayoral Candidates' stances][1] with regard to improving it.
