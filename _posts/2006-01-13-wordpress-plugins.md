Jackson [just migrated][1] to [WordPress][2].

I figured I'd give a rundown of the plugins I find useful, for his benefit and others:

  * [Brian's Threaded Comments][3] -- A nice plugin that allows for threaded comments, something I've always found necessary.
  * [Flash Filter Plus][4] -- This is one most probably won't need, but it's what I use to display the little flash applet with samples of music I'm reviewing.
  * [Live Comment Preview][5] -- Uses DHTML to display a live preview of comments at the bottom of the page as they are being typed.
  * [WordPress Feedburner Plugin][6] -- Facilitates redirection of your feed to feedburner.
  * [Google Sitemaps Generator][7] -- Automatically generates a google sitemap for your blog (Mine is [here][8])
  * [Spell Checker][9] -- I actually rarely use this, because I spell perfektly.</i> 
      * [WordVerify][10] -- The anti-comment-spam plugin that I wrote.</ul>
