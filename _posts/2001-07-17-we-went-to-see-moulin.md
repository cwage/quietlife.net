We went to see [Moulin Rouge][1], tonight (after I bought a bitchin new [overdrive pedal][2] at Mars Music, that is. woohoo!) and BOY did I think it ruled. I kindof want to see it again.

The cinematography and set design is pretty breathtaking, and [Ewan McGregor][3]'s actually got quite a voice on 'im.. Somehow I feel really cheated by the powers that be, knowing that this guy is famous, rich, goodlooking, has a Scottish accent, AND he can sing. I mean, come on. What's the catch? I bet he has really bad [halitosis][4], or something. Yeah, that's it.
