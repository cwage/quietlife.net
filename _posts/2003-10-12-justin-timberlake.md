Did anyone else see [Justin Timberlake][1] on SNL this week? I was actually sortof impressed with his first number. Amanda said he cites guys like [Michael Jackson][2] and [Donny Hathaway][3] (whose album [Live][4] is the single best live R&B album ever recorded, period) as his influences and it shows.. The second song was a much more stale and boring dance-pop type thing. I find myself wondering if [his album][5] is more like the former or the latter (I can only assume the latter). Anyone know?
