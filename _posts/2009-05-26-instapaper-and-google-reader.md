[This][1] is an insanely clever use of [Yahoo Pipes][2].. I just setup the same thing using google reader instead of delicious .. Now when I star an item in google reader, this yahoo pipes thing, when it fires, will automatically sync it over to instapaper, which I can update for offline reading with the iphone app. Pretty slick.
