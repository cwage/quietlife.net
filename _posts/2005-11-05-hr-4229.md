You should write your representative and ask them to support [H.R. 4229][1], which would [make the Plan B "morning after" pill available without a prescription.][2] The letter I sent to Jim Cooper:

> Jim,
> 
> Please consider supporting HR 4229. The FDA has stalled long enough on making this drug available, despite the objections of the public and the scientific community.
> 
> Thanks,
> 
> --Chris 

Short and sweet. Thanks to [womenshealthnews][3] to bringing this to my attention.
