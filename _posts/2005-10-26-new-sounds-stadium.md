So, via [Brittney at NiT][1], I see that [the Nashville Sounds][2] Stadium downtown [is a done deal][3].

This should be interesting, seeing as how the now vacant giant-field-of-sod where the stadium will go is right across 1st from my apartment complex. Here's hoping they manage to keep 1st open relatively clear of construction equipment, unlike Demonbreun, which has unfortunately been closed between 3rd and 4th for the duration of the Schermerhorn symphony hall.

I am still trying to figure out how they are going to fit an entire stadium on that tract of land.
