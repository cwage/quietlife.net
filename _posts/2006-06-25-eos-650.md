So, [Oleg][1] is going to sell me a Canon D30, which means I'll be diving headlong in to the world of DSLRs. However I still needed to dig up a lens.. Luckily, a friend of mine generously offered to send me his Canon EOS 650 for basically the cost of shipping, and I received it this week.

The lens is a basic zoom: 35-70mm 1:3.5-4.5. Since I'm impatient, while waiting for the D30, I've been taking the EOS 650 out and taking some pictures on actual film. The highlights (as such) are [here][2]. Taking pictures without instant feedback is hard.
