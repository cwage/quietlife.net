Amanda and I got the first disc of the first season of [Lost][1] from netflix today. We watched the 2 parts of the Pilot. I found it pretty entertaining, but I found myself wishing they didn't throw in all the freak-out mystery in the jungle. I mean, when a giant plane crashes off-course on a desert island in the middle of nowhere with 40-some survivors, do you really need supernatural shit in the jungle to make things interesting?
