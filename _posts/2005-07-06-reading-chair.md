I need a reading chair. I now appreciate this as a piece of furniture. A nice place with a handy lamp where you can sit up and read. I guess most people would have these in their study, or office, or library, or what have you.

Often I find myself choosing to sit in front of a computer and read blogs rather than reading any of the pile of books/articles I have in hardcopy to finish simply because I usually read in bed and would promptly fall asleep.

This of course is an indication that I should probably just **go to bed**, but hey.
