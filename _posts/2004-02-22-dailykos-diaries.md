For keeping your finger on the pulse of politics, there's nothing better than a conglomerate of political geeks, and therefore no site better than [dailykos.com][1], particularly [the Diaries][2].

But, as far as I can tell, there's no RSS feed for the diaries alone, and I hardly read anything these days if it doesn't have an RSS feed.

So, I wrote a perl script to scrape the RDF info from the main page and dump it into an [RSS feed][3].

It seems to be working okay so far. Feel free to use it. It's updated every 5 minutes.
