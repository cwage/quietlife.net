After talking to some friends, and spending 2+ hours trying to get [blogger.com][1] to post my template updates, I decided to give [Movable Type][2] a whirl. I was so impressed that I went ahead and switched over.

The benefits are that movable type is just a package that runs on your webserver.. You don't have to rely on anyone else to get to your data.. No more pounding on blogger.com trying to make an update to my webpage.

 [1]: http://www.blogger.com/
 [2]: http://www.movabletype.org/
