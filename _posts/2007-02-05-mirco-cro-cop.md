<img src="http://quietlife.net/images/filipovic.JPG" width="199" height="244" alt="mirco cro cop" style="float: right; padding-left: 10px;" />Saturday night, Nick and I went to watch the UFC fights. There were some good ones, but by far the one that stands out was not even a good fight. It was a massacre. This guy, Mirco Cro Cop Filipovic, is perhaps the single most intimidating human being I've ever seen in my entire life.

He stalked Eddie Sanchez the entire fight, never breaking a casual walk. He never broke a sweat. He never changed his facial expression even once, and he utterly destroyed Sanchez. It was hard to watch. He's like a real-life [Ivan Drago][1], complete with the eastern european accent.
