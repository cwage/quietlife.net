Woohoo! [David Byrne][1] is playing at the [Ryman Auditorium][2], probably one of Nashville's coolest music venues. Prior to becoming home of the [Grand Ole Opry][3], it opened its doors in 1892 as an opera (opry? get it?) house -- an architectual wonder designed by Tom Ryman. Eventually, though, the Grand Ole Opry began to outgrow the class of the Ryman Auditorium, and I also think there was some sort of fire-code violation problem involving the legal limit for number of sequined outfits allowed in one business-district building.

Anyhow, it's a really neat place -- I saw [Elvis Costello][4] there, and it was pretty amazing.
