I'd like to give a random shout-out to one of my favorite bloggers: [Les Jones][1]. We don't exactly see eye to eye on everything. In fact, come to think about it, I believe that if you check the record, in every area where we disagree, I am right and he's wrong. I used science to figure this out.

But what I love about his blog is that he posts about things that genuinely interest him. I think that's the recipe for true success with a blog. I've read a lot more interesting things, and had more thought-provoking (and argument-inducing) interactions on his blog than many of the "A-list" blogs I read. Actually, come to think of it, I don't read any "A-List" blogs anymore, but still.

Kudos, Les, and keep up the good work.
