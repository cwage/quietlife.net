What I've been listening to:

  * American Don -- [Don Caballero][1]'s <strike>new</strike> most recent album. I like it a lot. It's a little mellower, eliminating things that prevented me from really digging them in the past. It reminds me of [Slint][2].
  * Still fixated on the [new New Pornographers][3] album.
  * I like that [Gorillaz][4] song "Feel Good, Inc." more than I expected, since Gorillaz usually annoys the crap out of me. This in itself is an anomaly, since I like most of their constituent parts ([Cibo Matto][5], [Blur][6], [Dan the Automator][7]). I attribute most of my annoyance to [Del][8], who I generally can't stand, no matter how hard I try.
  * I heard two [Bloc Party][9] songs that are sorta okay.
  * Chuck Coleman's "Brian Played Guitar" is a clever and catchy song.
  * I grabbed a [Thievery Corporation][10] album from emusic for the hell of it and for the [David Byrne][11] song on it. So far my response is: meh.
  * I've been giving [Antony and the Johnsons][12] a listen -- he's worked with [Rufus Wainwright][13] I guess. It's not bad, but I am not sure I can get over his voice.

I think that's it.
