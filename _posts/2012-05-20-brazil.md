We watched [Brazil][1] the other night. It's been a while since I have seen it -- way before the [latest incarnation of Doctor Who][2]. I was struck by the vast quantity of similarities and seeming influence rooted in Brazil -- or at least the "sci fi noir" feel/genre in general. [Cassandra][3] at least seems to be a direct nod to (if not a not-so-subtle ripoff of) Sam's mother.

Side-note: Jack Lint would be a great halloween costume (and an even better dragoncon costume -- though it's probably been done). I researched the mask a bit and it's hard to find, but I found one guy that makes replicas for $150. A bit steep for a costume. Anyone out there in my vast readership have any 3d modeling experience? It seems like if you could find a decent straight-on shot with shadows (i.e. [this one][4]), you could pretty easily map the gradient to pull a z-axis out of it into a 3d model and then use a 3d printer to make it. But I have no idea how to do that. I only know how to talk smart, not do smart.
