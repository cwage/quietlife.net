It's our understanding that the authority to defer deportation for Claudia Nunez lies with Stella Jarina, the district director of the [New Orleans field office of the USCIS][1].

We have created [a petition][2], addressed to Stella Jarina, imploring her to reconsider the case. Please give it a look, and hopefully, your signature!
