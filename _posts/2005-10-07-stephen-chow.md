I am pretty much in love with Stephen Chow.

Amanda had the great wisdom and foresight to buy [Kung Fu Hustle][1] on DVD, and so we watched it again tonight.

I am going to just throw this out here: Best Kung-Fu Movie Ever.

Now, I know there are purists out there that will argue that maybe this movie isn't pure kung-fu and is thus ineligible, and that's fine, but I think we'd at least agree that it took the art to the next level and made it even awesomer.

That said, I liked [Shaolin Soccer][2] **even better** -- which, I guess, makes it the top dog. I need to buy this one as well.
