These aren't really reviews, I lied. We saw three movies over the last week or so:

[Pirates of the Caribbean][1]: meh. I think that the rest of the world apparently finds Johnny Depp prancing around as a pirate a lot cuter than I do. It was okay, but not great.

[A Scanner Darkly][2]: Disclaimer: I haven't read the PKD book. I found it to be slow and boring through the beginning and middle -- sortof like [True Romance][3], except not funny. The ending was good. The "animation" was a stupid, distracting gimmick that I didn't care for.

[Oldboy][4]: One of the best movies I've seen in a while -- and one of the most messed up. Highly recommended.
